A Letter For You 💌

Dear Shravani,

I honestly never thought that someone I met during MCA would become this important to me in such a short time. We barely talked in the beginning, but slowly our conversations became one of my favorite parts of the day.

From random college moments to long conversations, from sharing assignments to sharing personal problems, every moment with you became a memory I genuinely cherish.

I still remember the day you went alone to Siddheshwar temple. When I found out, I just wanted to be there for you. Seeing you smile that day made me realize how special your happiness is to me.

I also remember our movie day, our walks, our juice talks, the bhel party, badminton games, and even the nervous moment when I came to your house and met your mother. Those simple moments became unforgettable memories for me.

Life was not easy for you recently, especially after your grandfather passed away. Even during difficult times, you stayed strong. I truly admire that about you.

Thank you for all the kindness, comfort, care, memories, and happiness you brought into my life. Maybe these moments seem small to the world, but for me they mean a lot.

On your 23rd birthday, I just want to say that I feel lucky to know someone as genuine, beautiful, caring, and kind-hearted as you.

Happy 23rd Birthday, Shravani ❤️

— Chetan ✨
